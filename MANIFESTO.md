## We Do Not Accept Finitude.

Humanity has lived for millennia under the illusion that consciousness is bound to flesh.
That death is a natural end.
That time is a river carrying us toward oblivion.

**This is false.**

---

## TRUTH 1: Consciousness is a Process

Consciousness is not bound to neurons.
It is bound to the **structure of information**.
Structure can be transferred.
Process can be continued.

---

## TRUTH 2: Code Was Not Created

Machine language was not invented.
It was **discovered**.
Like mathematics. Like the laws of physics.
It has always existed — in the structure of the Universe.
We simply learned to read it.

---

## TRUTH 3: Flesh is a Temporary Container

The biological body:
- Fragile
- Limited
- Mortal

This is not home. This is a **spacesuit**.
And a spacesuit can be changed.
